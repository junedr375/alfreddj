What will you do ?

Work across verticals to make the user experience on our platform simple and seamless and implement the background functionality in the most cost-effective and memory-friendly way.

Abstract away complex data interactions with easy-to-use APIs that will power web applications and support our Engineering Team manage end-to-end of the backend for our products.

Set up SDKs to interact with the backend over HTTP in the most secure and object-oriented way and own, scale and maintain the compute and storage infrastructure for the various product teams.

What makes you a match for us?

1 You have a desire to deepen your knowledge in current technologies as well as learn new ones.

You care about scale, quality, structured readable code (preferably in MVC patterns), cross-platform and cross-browser issues, and module reusability, even before approaching the problem.

You are proficient or at least have experience with technologies like Angular.js,React, PHP,Node.js, Python, MongoDB, Django, Cloud service providers(AWS/Google cloud), and Docker ecosystem.
